The School Day

The school day at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is carefully structured to maximize learning time while ensuring student well-being and maintaining Islamic principles. This guide outlines the typical daily schedule and expectations.

Daily Schedule

  • 7:30 AM: School gates open, students arrive
  • 8:00 AM: Prompt assembly attendance required
  • 8:20 AM - 12:00 PM: Morning lessons (including breaks)
  • 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch break
  • 1:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Afternoon lessons
  • 3:30 PM: End of school day
  • 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Afterschool activities and clubs

Lesson Periods

The school day is divided into periods of approximately 40-45 minutes with short breaks between lessons to allow students to move between classes and refresh.

Break Times

  • Morning Break: Mid-morning refreshment (10-15 minutes)
  • Lunch Break: Noon to 1:00 PM - Students may eat packed lunch or purchase from school canteen

Prayer Times

Islamic prayer times are accommodated during the school day:

  • Zuhr (midday prayer) - During lunch break
  • Asr (afternoon prayer) - Facilitated after school if needed
  • Students have access to prayer facilities

Student Expectations

During the school day, students are expected to:

  • Arrive on time in proper uniform
  • Attend all classes and activities
  • Participate actively in lessons
  • Maintain discipline and respect
  • Follow school rules and regulations
  • Keep the school clean and tidy

Supervision

Students are supervised throughout the day by:

  • Class teachers during lessons
  • Break duty staff during breaks
  • Activity sponsors during afterschool programs

Early Dismissal

Students may only be dismissed early with parental permission and through proper notification to the school office. Medical emergencies will be handled immediately.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
