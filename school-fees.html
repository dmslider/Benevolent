School Fees

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing affordable, quality education. Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and reasonable while ensuring we maintain our high standards of teaching and facilities.

Fee Structure

Please note: The following is a general fee structure. Current and updated fees should be obtained from the school office.

Tuition Fees

  • Junior Level (Forms 1-3): Contact school office for current rates
  • Senior Level (Forms 4-5): Contact school office for current rates
  • Sixth Form: Contact school office for current rates

Additional Fees

  • Development/Facilities Levy
  • Technology & IT Resources Fee
  • Examination Fees
  • Library & Learning Resources Fee
  • Sports & Activities Fee

Payment Terms

  • Fees are payable in full at the beginning of each school year
  • Installment payment options may be available upon request
  • Fees must be paid before students can register for examinations
  • Late payment may result in suspension from school

Payment Methods

  • Direct bank transfer to school account
  • Cash payment at the school office
  • Check payment (where applicable)

Financial Assistance

We recognize that education is a right and some families may need financial support. The school offers:

  • Scholarships & Bursaries for deserving students
  • Payment plans to ease financial burden
  • Assistance for students in genuine financial hardship

Refund Policy

Refunds are subject to the following conditions:

  • Refund requests must be made in writing to the school principal
  • Refunds for withdrawal before mid-term may be subject to administrative fees
  • No refunds are given after mid-term
  • Any outstanding balance must be settled before refunds are processed

Financial Transparency

The school is committed to transparent use of school fees. Funds are used for:

  • Staff salaries and professional development
  • Infrastructure maintenance and improvement
  • Learning resources and technology
  • Student support services
  • Utilities and operational costs

Contact Finance Office

For specific information about fees, payment arrangements, or financial assistance, please contact the school finance office or get in touch with us.
