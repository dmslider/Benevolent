How to Apply

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for applying to Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. Follow these steps to complete your application process.

Application Steps

Step 1: Obtain Application Form

  • Visit the school during office hours to collect an application form
  • Alternatively, request a form by calling the school office
  • Forms can be printed from our website if available

Step 2: Complete the Application Form

Fill in all required information:

  • Personal details (name, date of birth, contact information)
  • Parent/guardian information
  • Previous school information
  • Academic history and achievements
  • Any special talents or interests

Step 3: Gather Required Documents

Prepare the following documents:

  • Birth certificate or national identification
  • Recent school report cards or transcripts
  • Character reference letter from previous school
  • Passport-sized photographs (2 copies)
  • Medical examination records (if applicable)

Step 4: Submit Application

  • Complete the form accurately and legibly
  • Attach all required documents
  • Submit to the school admissions office
  • Note the submission date and obtain a receipt

Step 5: Entrance Assessment

Qualified applicants will be notified of the examination date and time. The entrance assessment includes:

  • Mathematics test (60 minutes)
  • English Language test (60 minutes)
  • Reasoning/Logic test (45 minutes)

Step 6: Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents will be invited for an interview. This is an opportunity for the school to learn more about the student and family.

Step 7: Admission Decision

Applicants will be notified of admission decisions within 2 weeks of the interview. Admitted students must then proceed with registration and fee payment.

Application Deadlines

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. However, early application is encouraged as spaces are limited. Contact the admissions office for current application deadlines.

Application Fees

A non-refundable application fee may be required. Please inquire at the admissions office for current fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When can I apply?
A: Applications are accepted throughout the year for the next academic session. Early application is recommended.

Q: What is the minimum age requirement?
A: Students should be at least 11 years old for Form 1 admission.

Q: Can I reapply if I am not admitted?
A: Yes, students may reapply in subsequent years if they wish.

Contact Admissions Office

For any questions about the application process, please contact us or visit during office hours.
