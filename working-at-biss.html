Working at BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is a leading educational institution seeking passionate, qualified professionals to join our team. We offer rewarding career opportunities for educators and support staff committed to excellence in Islamic education.

Why Work at BISS?

Join our dedicated team and enjoy:

  • Supportive and collaborative work environment
  • Commitment to professional development and growth
  • Opportunities to make a difference in students' lives
  • Alignment with Islamic values in education
  • Competitive remuneration packages
  • Professional recognition and career advancement
  • Access to modern teaching resources and facilities

Current Vacancies

We regularly recruit for various positions including:

  • Subject teachers (Mathematics, English, Sciences, Humanities)
  • Islamic Studies educators
  • Information Technology specialists
  • Administrative staff
  • Support staff (library, laboratory, IT support)

For information about current job openings, please contact the school office or check back regularly.

Qualifications & Requirements

To join our team, candidates should possess:

  • Relevant educational qualifications (Bachelor's degree minimum)
  • Professional certifications or credentials
  • Experience in education or related field
  • Commitment to Islamic values and education
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Passion for student development and learning

Application Process

Interested candidates should:

  • Submit a detailed curriculum vitae (CV)
  • Provide copies of relevant qualifications
  • Include references from previous employers
  • Write a cover letter expressing interest
  • Submit to the school office or via email

Staff Support & Benefits

The school supports staff through:

  • Professional development programs
  • Training and workshops
  • Mentoring and coaching
  • Health and welfare support
  • Pension schemes

Contact & Inquiries

For inquiries about employment opportunities or to submit applications, please contact us or visit the school office during business hours.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
