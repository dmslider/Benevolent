Our Professional Staff

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is fortunate to have a dedicated team of professional educators and support staff committed to student success. Our staff members are qualified, experienced, and passionate about providing excellent education rooted in Islamic values.

Staff Categories

Teaching Staff

Our teaching staff includes qualified educators with expertise across all subject areas:

  • Bachelor's degrees in specialist subjects
  • Professional teaching qualifications
  • Secondary education experience
  • Continuous professional development
  • Commitment to student welfare

School Leadership

Our leadership team provides strategic direction and oversight:

  • Head Teacher – Overall school leadership
  • Senior Management Team – Academic oversight
  • Form Tutors – Pastoral care and coordination
  • Subject Coordinators – Curriculum leadership
  • Deputy Heads – Operational management

Support Staff

Our support team ensures smooth school operations:

  • Administrative and secretarial staff
  • Librarian and library assistants
  • IT technician and support
  • Laboratory technicians
  • Catering and maintenance personnel

Professional Development & Support

Continuous Learning

The school invests in staff development through:

  • Regular professional development workshops
  • Subject specialist training programs
  • Educational conference attendance
  • Curriculum development initiatives
  • Mentoring and coaching programs

Staff Well-being

We prioritize the well-being of our staff:

  • Supportive working environment
  • Recognition of achievements
  • Professional support services
  • Work-life balance initiatives
  • Health and wellness programs

Professional Standards & Code of Conduct

All staff members adhere to a professional code of conduct that emphasizes:

Integrity & Honesty

Acting with truthfulness and uprightness in all professional interactions

Professionalism

Maintaining high standards of competence and respect in all duties

Student Welfare

Prioritizing safety and well-being of all students at all times

Confidentiality

Protecting sensitive information and maintaining appropriate boundaries

Continuous Improvement

Seeking opportunities for growth and enhanced practice

Islamic Values

Embodying Islamic principles of respect, honesty, and service
