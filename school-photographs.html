School Photographs

Photography is an important part of documenting and celebrating school life at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. Professional school photographs are taken annually to commemorate student achievements and school memories.

Annual School Photography

Professional photographers visit the school once annually to take:

  • Individual student portraits
  • Class group photographs
  • Staff team photographs
  • Special occasion group photos

Photography Schedule

Photography sessions are typically held in the first term of the academic year. Specific dates and arrangements are communicated to parents and students in advance.

Dress Code for Photography

All students must wear full school uniform during photography sessions, unless otherwise instructed for special occasion photos.

Ordering Photographs

Parents will receive information about ordering photographs from the professional photographer. Orders are placed directly with the photography company.

School Memories & Yearbook

School photographs are used in:

  • School yearbook (if available)
  • School notice boards and displays
  • School archives and records
  • Promotional materials (with permission)

Privacy & Permissions

Parents provide permission for photography during enrollment. Any additional use of student images requires explicit parental consent. The school respects student privacy and Islamic values regarding photography.

Event Photography

The school also takes photographs during school events and celebrations. These may be displayed on the school notice board or website (with appropriate permissions).

Contact for Photography Queries

For questions about school photography, please contact us or visit the school office.
