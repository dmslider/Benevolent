Sports & Athletics

At Benevolent Islamic Secondary School, we believe physical education and sports are integral parts of student development. Our comprehensive sports program promotes fitness, teamwork, discipline, and healthy competition.

Sports Programs

Football

Our football program includes inter-house competitions and matches against other schools. Training is conducted by experienced coaches to develop both skills and tactical understanding.

Athletics

Track and field events including sprinting, long distance running, jumping, and throwing. Students compete at house and school levels throughout the year.

Basketball

A growing program with both boys and girls teams. Basketball develops coordination, teamwork, and strategic thinking.

Volleyball

Team sport that builds coordination and communication skills. Regular training sessions and inter-house competitions.

Physical Education

All students participate in regular PE lessons as part of the curriculum. PE develops fitness, coordination, and respect for physical wellness in line with Islamic teachings.

Sports Facilities

  • Football pitch for training and matches
  • Basketball court with professional markings
  • Athletics track for running and field events
  • Sports hall for indoor activities
  • Changing facilities and equipment storage

Participation & Opportunities

We encourage all students to participate in sports regardless of their skill level. Whether you are interested in competitive sports or recreational activity, there is something for everyone. Trials for sports teams are held at the beginning of each term.

Achievements

Our students have achieved notable success in inter-school competitions. We celebrate both individual excellence and team achievements. Participation and improvement are highly valued.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
