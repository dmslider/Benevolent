Admissions

Welcome to Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. We are pleased to invite qualified students to apply for admission. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to our values of excellence, integrity, and Islamic principles.

Why Choose BISS?

  • World-class education rooted in Islamic values
  • Highly qualified and dedicated teaching staff
  • Modern facilities and learning resources
  • Comprehensive support for student development
  • Strong track record of academic excellence
  • Vibrant extracurricular programs and activities

Entry Requirements

Students seeking admission to Benevolent Islamic Secondary School must:

  • Be a minimum of 11 years old for Junior Level admission
  • Possess basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Demonstrate commitment to learning and school values
  • Have completed primary education or equivalent
  • Meet any specific subject prerequisites for specialized programs

Admissions Process

Step 1: Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents. See How to Apply for detailed instructions.

Step 2: Entrance Assessment

Qualified applicants will sit for entrance examinations in Mathematics, English, and Reasoning.

Step 3: Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents are invited for a formal interview with school leadership.

Step 4: Admission Decision

Applicants will be notified of admission decisions within two weeks of the interview.

Step 5: Registration

Admitted students complete registration and payment of school fees.

Financial Information

Information about School Fees and payment schedules is available on our fees page. We also offer Scholarships & Bursaries for deserving students.

Contact Admissions Office

For inquiries about admissions, please contact us or visit the admissions office during school hours.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
