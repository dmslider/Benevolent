Information for Parents

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School values the partnership between school and home. This section provides important information and resources for parents to support their children's education and success.

Key Resources for Parents

  • Term Dates - School calendar and schedules
  • Uniform Requirements - Dress code and where to purchase
  • School Fees - Fee structure and payment options
  • Parent-Teacher Consultations - Meeting schedules
  • School Policies - Rules and expectations

Parent Involvement

We encourage parent involvement through:

  • Attending school events and celebrations
  • Participating in parent-teacher meetings
  • Supporting school fundraising activities
  • Volunteering for school projects
  • Engaging in regular home-school communication

Academic Support at Home

Parents can support their child's learning by:

  • Creating a quiet study environment
  • Ensuring adequate sleep and nutrition
  • Monitoring homework completion
  • Maintaining regular contact with teachers
  • Encouraging reading and learning beyond school
  • Discussing school experiences and challenges

Student Welfare & Safety

The school is committed to student welfare and safety. Parents should:

  • Report any concerns about student well-being
  • Ensure timely medical treatment for health issues
  • Communicate any special needs or accommodations required
  • Support the school's discipline policies

Financial Matters

Please ensure:

  • School fees are paid on time
  • All financial obligations are met
  • Financial difficulties are communicated to the school
  • Receipts are retained for all payments

Contact & Communication

To get in touch with the school or discuss any matter, please contact us during office hours or visit in person.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
