School Policies

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School operates under a comprehensive set of policies designed to ensure a safe, supportive, and conducive learning environment for all students. These policies reflect our commitment to academic excellence, Islamic values, and student welfare.

Key Policy Areas

Academic Policies

  • Assessment and examination policies
  • Academic integrity and honesty
  • Grading and promotion criteria
  • Special educational needs support
  • Credit/admission requirements

Student Conduct & Discipline

  • Code of conduct and behavioral expectations
  • Uniform and appearance standards
  • Punctuality and attendance requirements
  • Disciplinary procedures and sanctions
  • Anti-bullying and harassment policies

Safety & Welfare

  • Child safeguarding policy
  • Health and safety regulations
  • Infection control and hygiene
  • Emergency procedures
  • Student support and counseling

Communication & Engagement

  • Parent-school communication policy
  • Student grievance procedures
  • Complaint resolution process
  • Privacy and data protection

Financial Policies

  • School fees and payment procedures
  • Financial aid and scholarships
  • Refund policy

Policy Compliance

All students, staff, and parents are expected to:

  • Understand and comply with school policies
  • Support the implementation of policies
  • Report policy violations appropriately

Policy Review

School policies are reviewed regularly to ensure they remain relevant, fair, and effective. Updates are communicated to the school community.

Accessing Policies

Comprehensive copies of school policies are available:

  • At the school office
  • From your student's form tutor
  • Upon request from school management

Policy Questions

If you have questions about any school policy, please contact us or speak with school management.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
