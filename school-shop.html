School Shop

The Benevolent Islamic Secondary School Shop provides students, parents, and staff with essential school materials, uniforms, and supplies. Our shop is conveniently located within the school premises.

Available Items

Uniforms

Complete school uniform items including:

  • White shirts (short and long sleeve options)
  • Black trousers (various sizes)
  • Black skirts for female students
  • School ties
  • PE kit components
  • Winter jumpers and blazers

Stationery & Books

  • Exercise books and notepads
  • Pens, pencils, and writing instruments
  • Rulers, erasers, and other drafting tools
  • Textbooks and recommended reading materials
  • Art and craft supplies

School Supplies

  • School bags and backpacks
  • Water bottles and lunch containers
  • Name tags and labels
  • Computer consumables and accessories

Pricing & Payment

All items are priced affordably and competitively. The shop accepts cash payments. Bulk orders and special requests can be accommodated with advance notice.

Ordering & Bulk Purchases

For large orders, especially uniforms, parents and students are encouraged to place advance orders to ensure availability and potentially receive discounts. Contact the school shop staff for bulk order arrangements.

Operating Hours

Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 3:30 PM
Saturday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Closed: Sundays and public holidays

Quality & Standards

All uniforms and supplies sold in the school shop meet the school's quality standards and specifications. We ensure that products are durable, appropriate, and suitable for school use.

Contact Information

For inquiries about specific items, availability, or bulk orders, please visit the school shop or contact the school office during business hours.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
