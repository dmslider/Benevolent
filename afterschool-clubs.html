Afterschool Clubs & Activities

Afterschool clubs are an important part of student life at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. These programs provide opportunities for students to explore interests, develop new skills, and build friendships outside the regular curriculum.

Available Clubs

Academic Clubs

  • Debate and Public Speaking
  • Science Club
  • Mathematics Society
  • Technology & Programming Club
  • Environmental Club

Cultural & Arts Clubs

  • Art & Design Club
  • Drama & Theater Group
  • Music Club
  • Poetry & Creative Writing Club

Sports Clubs

  • Football Club
  • Basketball Club
  • Athletics Club
  • Volleyball Club

Service & Faith Clubs

  • Islamic Society
  • Community Service Club
  • Quranic Studies Circle

Club Benefits

  • Explore new interests and talents
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Build lasting friendships with students from other year groups
  • Engage in fun and meaningful activities
  • Enhance personal development

Participation

All students are encouraged to join clubs of their interest. Most clubs meet 2-3 times per week after school. Registration typically occurs at the beginning of each academic term.

Club Leadership

Each club is led by a staff sponsor and student committee, providing leadership development opportunities for students.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
