About Benevolent Islamic Secondary School

WELCOME TO BENEVOLENT ISLAMIC SECONDARY SCHOOL

A very warm welcome to you as you peruse through our website. We hope that you will find it quite interesting and informative. We have endeavoured to make this website very attractive as well.To provide comprehensive, high-quality secondary education rooted in Islamic values and principles, empowering students to become responsible, knowledgeable, and ethically-grounded citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Sierra Leone recognized for academic excellence, moral integrity, and the development of well-rounded individuals prepared for higher education and meaningful contributions to society.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Integrity: Building character through honesty and ethical conduct
  • Faith: Strengthening Islamic principles and spiritual growth
  • Community: Fostering collaboration and social responsibility
  • Innovation: Embracing modern educational approaches

Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • Age Requirement: Students must be of appropriate age for their form level
  • Academic Records: Certified copies of previous school records and transcripts
  • Entrance Examination: Successful completion of entrance test in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge
  • Interview: Personal interview with admissions committee
  • Birth Certificate: Certified copy of birth certificate
  • Character Reference: Letter of recommendation from previous school headmaster
  • Health Certificate: Proof of vaccinations and general health screening
  • Parent/Guardian Information: Completed contact forms and identification
