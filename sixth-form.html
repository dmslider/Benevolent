Sixth Form Programme

Our Sixth Form represents the pinnacle of secondary education at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. This two-year post-secondary programme is designed for academically accomplished students who wish to pursue advanced studies and prepare for university-level education. The Sixth Form combines rigorous academic study with personal development, preparing students to become leaders and innovators in their chosen fields.

Programme Overview

The Sixth Form curriculum follows the British A-Level/IB system, offering students the opportunity to specialize in subjects of their choice. This allows for deep, focused study that develops genuine expertise and prepares students for higher education. Students benefit from smaller class sizes, more interactive teaching, and greater independence in their learning.

Admission Requirements

To gain admission to our Sixth Form, students must:

  • Have completed the Senior Level programme successfully
  • Achieve strong grades in WASSCE or equivalent examinations
  • Demonstrate commitment to academic excellence and personal development
  • Show readiness for advanced independent study
  • Meet specified minimum grades in subjects they wish to study

Subject Specialization

Students typically select three to four subjects from a wide range of options, allowing them to pursue their interests and career aspirations:

  • Sciences: Advanced Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Humanities: English Literature, History, Geography, Government
  • Languages: Arabic, French, Applied Language Studies
  • Technical: Information Technology, Computing, Engineering
  • Specialised: Economics, Business Studies, Islamic Studies, Philosophy

Academic Excellence

Our Sixth Form is renowned for academic achievement:

  • Experienced faculty with specialist knowledge in their subjects
  • Small group teaching allowing personalized attention and discussion-based learning
  • University-style tutorials and independent research projects
  • Regular progress monitoring and academic mentoring
  • Extensive preparation for university entrance examinations

University Preparation and Career Guidance

A cornerstone of the Sixth Form experience is comprehensive preparation for university entry and career development:

  • University application support and guidance
  • Personal statement writing workshops
  • Interview preparation sessions
  • Visiting lectures from university representatives
  • Career counselling and professional development
  • Work experience and internship opportunities

Leadership and Enrichment

Sixth Form students are encouraged to take on leadership roles and engage in enrichment activities:

  • Sixth Form Council and student leadership opportunities
    • \n
  • Mentoring of junior students
  • Participation in academic competitions and debates
  • Community service and social responsibility projects
  • Cultural and sporting events

Pastoral Care and Wellbeing

While academically demanding, the Sixth Form provides strong pastoral support. Form tutors and counsellors work with students to manage stress, maintain wellbeing, and ensure they develop into balanced, mature individuals ready for the challenges of university and professional life.
