Student Leadership: Head Boy & Head Girl

The positions of Head Boy and Head Girl represent the pinnacle of student leadership at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. These esteemed roles are held by exceptional students who embody our core values of academic excellence, character, integrity, and service. The Head Boy and Head Girl serve as ambassadors of the school and leaders of the entire student body.

Leadership Roles & Responsibilities

Student Representation

Represent student interests and voice to school management and leadership

School Ambassadors

Serve as faces of the school at official events, welcoming visitors and ceremonies

Ceremonial Leadership

Lead school assemblies, read lessons at special events, and present awards

Student Council

Chair student council meetings and oversee student government initiatives

Community Service

Lead school-wide charitable initiatives and meaningful community projects

Student Mentorship

Mentor younger students and provide academic and pastoral guidance

Democratic Selection & Election Process

The selection of Head Boy and Head Girl is a rigorous and transparent democratic process that ensures we select the most qualified and capable student leaders:

Eligibility & Announcement

Candidates must be in the final year (Form 4 or Sixth Form) with excellent academic standing and demonstrated strong character

Nomination Phase

Students can nominate themselves or be nominated by peers who believe in their leadership potential

Application Submission

Candidates submit detailed applications outlining their vision, leadership plans, and goals for the school

Interviews

Shortlisted candidates are interviewed by school leadership and staff to assess readiness

Student Election

The entire school community votes democratically to elect the Head Boy and Head Girl

Formal Installation

Elected leaders are formally installed in their positions during a special assembly ceremony

Essential Leadership Qualities

Outstanding Head Boys and Head Girls typically possess:

Academic Excellence

Strong intellectual ability and commitment to academic achievement

Moral Character

Ethical integrity rooted firmly in Islamic values and principles

Communication Skills

Confidence and excellence in public speaking and interpersonal communication

Empathy

Genuine compassion and empathy for their peers and their needs

Inspiration

Ability to inspire and motivate others to achieve their best

Responsibility

Strong sense of responsibility, reliability, and accountability

Humility

Humility and genuine willingness to serve others and the community

Vision

Clear vision for positive change and continuous school improvement

Leadership Development & Support

We invest significantly in developing our Head Boy and Head Girl to ensure they succeed:

Professional Training

  • Formal leadership training and development programmes
  • Regular mentoring from school principal and senior staff
  • Communication and public speaking coaching
  • Exposure to school governance and decision-making

Recognition & Legacy

  • Special insignia and regalia in school colours
  • Special privileges and school-wide recognition
  • Names commemorated in school records and history
  • Often go on to leadership in university and professional life
