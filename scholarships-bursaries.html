Scholarships & Bursaries

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to making quality education accessible to deserving students regardless of financial circumstances. We offer a range of scholarships and bursaries to support talented and underprivileged students.

Types of Financial Support

Academic Scholarships

Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievement:

  • Covers full or partial tuition fees
  • Based on entrance examination performance
  • Renewable based on continued academic excellence
  • May include additional support for learning materials

Sports Scholarships

For students with exceptional athletic talent:

  • Partial or full tuition support
  • Support for participation in school sports teams
  • Access to specialized coaching
  • Contingent on continued participation and discipline

Need-Based Bursaries

For students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds:

  • Partial or full tuition fee coverage
  • Based on family financial assessment
  • Assessed on annual basis
  • May include support for uniforms and materials

Orphan & Special Circumstance Support

For students facing special hardships:

  • Special consideration for orphaned students
  • Support for students in foster care
  • Consideration for students from displaced families
  • Comprehensive support packages tailored to circumstances

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must meet entrance examination requirements
  • Must demonstrate good conduct and discipline
  • Must be a citizen or legal resident of Sierra Leone
  • For need-based awards: must provide evidence of financial hardship
  • For sports scholarships: must excel in designated sports

Application Process

Step 1: Determine Eligibility

Identify which scholarship or bursary programs you qualify for based on your circumstances.

Step 2: Gather Required Documents

  • Application form (available from admissions office)
  • Recent school transcripts
  • For need-based: evidence of family financial situation
  • For sports: athletic achievement records
  • Letter of recommendation from school principal or teacher

Step 3: Submit Application

Submit completed application to the school admissions office by the published deadline.

Step 4: Assessment & Interview

The scholarship committee will review applications and may conduct interviews with shortlisted candidates.

Step 5: Award Notification

Successful applicants will be notified of scholarship awards and the terms of their awards.

Scholarship Terms

  • Awards are typically for one academic year and renewable upon meeting criteria
  • Recipients must maintain good academic standing
  • Recipients must adhere to school discipline policies
  • Athletes must actively participate in their sport
  • Terms and conditions will be specified in the award letter

Application Timeline

Scholarship applications are typically accepted from January to March each year. Contact the admissions office for exact deadlines.

Contact Information

For more information about scholarships and bursaries, please contact us or visit the school office.
