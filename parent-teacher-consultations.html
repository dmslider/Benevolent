Parent-Teacher Consultations

Effective communication between parents and teachers is essential for student success. Benevolent Islamic Secondary School organizes regular parent-teacher consultation sessions to discuss student progress, address concerns, and build partnerships in supporting student development.

Consultation Schedule

Parent-teacher consultations are held at regular intervals throughout the school year:

  • Term 1 Consultations: Mid-Term (October)
  • Term 2 Consultations: Mid-Term (January)
  • Term 3 Consultations: End of Year (May/June)
  • Emergency Consultations: Available as needed

Purpose of Consultations

Parent-teacher meetings provide opportunities to:

  • Discuss student academic progress and achievement
  • Share observations about student behavior and character
  • Identify areas for improvement and support
  • Plan strategies for enhancing student learning
  • Address concerns and questions
  • Celebrate student successes
  • Build strong home-school partnerships

Preparation for Consultations

Parents should:

  • Review recent school reports and assessments
  • Prepare questions or concerns to discuss
  • Arrive on time for scheduled meetings
  • Bring their student (where appropriate)

Teachers prepare:

  • Student progress reports and grades
  • Examples of student work
  • Observations and feedback
  • Recommendations for support

Communication Beyond Consultations

We encourage ongoing communication through:

  • Email communication with teachers
  • Phone calls regarding urgent matters
  • School announcements and newsletters
  • School website and portal updates
  • Informal chats at appropriate times

Scheduling Consultations

Scheduled consultation times are published well in advance. Parents should register with their student's form tutor to book appointment slots with subject teachers.

Special Consultations

If you have concerns requiring urgent discussion, please contact the school office to schedule a special meeting with your student's teacher or school leadership.
