Academic Programs

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School offers a comprehensive and rigorous academic curriculum designed to challenge and inspire our students. Our academic programs combine traditional subjects with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Curriculum Structure

Our curriculum is organized into three main sections:

  • Junior Level Guide: Foundation years focusing on core subjects and skill development
  • Senior Level Guide: Advanced curriculum preparing students for examination success
  • Sixth Form: Post-secondary education for specialized study and higher education preparation

Teaching Approach

Our teaching methodology emphasizes:

  • Student-centered learning and active engagement
  • Development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Integration of technology in the classroom
  • Regular assessment and feedback for continuous improvement
  • Collaborative learning through group projects and discussions

Learning Facilities

We provide students with access to:

  • Well-equipped Computer Laboratory with modern technology
  • Comprehensive School Library with extensive learning resources
  • Science laboratories for practical experimentation
  • Modern classrooms with interactive learning tools

Assessment & Excellence

Students are regularly assessed through various methods including continuous assessment, class tests, and formal examinations. We are proud of our Recent Public Results Stats which demonstrate the academic achievements of our students. Our commitment to excellence ensures that students achieve their full potential.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
