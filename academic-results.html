Recent Public Results Statistics

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is proud of the consistent academic achievements of our students in public examinations. Our results reflect the dedication of our teaching staff, commitment of our students, and comprehensive academic support systems.

Examination Performance

Our students regularly achieve excellent results in national examinations including WASSCE and other standardized assessments. The school maintains a strong track record of:

  • High pass rates across all subjects
  • Consistent subject excellence in core subjects
  • Improved student performance year-on-year
  • Strong performance in science and mathematics
  • Excellent language arts achievements

Success Stories

Our graduates go on to pursue:

  • University education at local and international institutions
  • Professional and vocational training
  • Successful careers across various sectors
  • Leadership positions in their communities

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuous improvement through:

  • Regular analysis of examination results
  • Implementation of enhanced teaching strategies
  • Individual student support and tutoring
  • Professional development for teaching staff
  • Investment in learning resources and technology

Achievement Recognition

We celebrate our students' achievements through:

  • School award ceremonies
  • Recognition in school publications
  • Award of certificates and commendations
  • Feature in school announcements

For detailed statistical information about specific examination results, please contact the academic office or school administration.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
