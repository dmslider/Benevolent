School Uniform

Uniforms are a vital part of our school identity and community spirit at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School. Our uniform dress code is designed to promote equality, discipline, and pride in belonging to our institution.

Uniform Requirements

Standard Uniform

  • Trousers: Black trousers (formal style, properly fitted)
  • Shirt: White shirt (short or long sleeves)
  • Tie: School tie (compulsory for formal occasions)
  • Shoes: Black formal shoes
  • Socks: Black or white socks

PE Kit

  • School PE shirt or plain white shirt
  • Black shorts or black joggers
  • Training shoes (white or black)
  • Sports socks

For Female Students

  • Black trousers or black skirt (knee-length)
  • White shirt
  • School tie (for formal occasions)
  • Black formal shoes

Uniform Standards

All students are expected to wear their uniform in a neat, clean, and well-maintained condition. The uniform should be worn correctly and with pride. Any damage to the uniform should be promptly repaired or replaced.

Where to Purchase

Uniforms can be purchased from the school shop or authorized suppliers. For details on pricing, sizing, and ordering, please visit the School Shop page or contact the school office.

Uniform Policy

Wearing the correct uniform is mandatory for all students. Students not in proper uniform may be asked to return home to change. Repeated uniform violations may result in disciplinary action. Parents are encouraged to ensure their children adhere to the uniform code as it is an essential part of our school's discipline and identity.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
