About Benevolent Islamic Secondary School

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing world-class secondary education rooted in Islamic values and principles. Our mission is to nurture intellectually curious, morally upright, and socially responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of learning that combines academic excellence with strong Islamic values, producing graduates who are equipped to face global challenges with integrity, wisdom, and compassion.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Providing high-quality education that develops critical thinking and intellectual growth
  • Fostering Islamic values, moral character, and ethical leadership
  • Promoting a supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Empowering students with skills for academic success and personal development
  • Engaging with parents and community in the educational process

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education and personal development.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct in all our dealings.

Respect

We respect diversity and treat all members of our community with dignity and consideration.

Faith

We integrate Islamic teachings and values into all aspects of school life.

School Facilities

  • State-of-the-art classrooms with modern learning resources
  • Well-equipped computer laboratory with internet connectivity
  • Comprehensive school library with extensive collection of resources
  • Sports facilities including football pitch and basketball court
  • Assembly hall for school events and gatherings
  • Mosque for Islamic prayers and spiritual development
