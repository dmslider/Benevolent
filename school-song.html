Our School Song

The Benevolent Islamic Secondary School Song is one of our most cherished traditions, sung at school assemblies, special events, and celebrations throughout the year. Our school song embodies our values, our history, and our commitment to excellence, and it serves as a unifying force that brings the entire BISS community together.

Our Motto: "God and My Right"

At the heart of our school identity is our powerful motto, "God and My Right." This phrase reflects our dual commitment to Islamic faith and individual integrity. It encapsulates our belief that true education combines spiritual development with personal responsibility, and that success comes through honouring both our faith and our principles.

The School Song Lyrics

The complete lyrics to our school song, typically sung during important gatherings and ceremonies, celebrate our commitment to excellence, character, and community service. The song's melody is distinctive and memorable, making it easy for students of all ages to sing together with pride and unity.

The Song's Significance

Our school song carries deep meaning for everyone in the BISS community:

  • School Identity: The song expresses who we are as an institution and what we stand for
  • Shared Heritage: It connects current students with generations of alumni who have sung the same song
  • Unity and Belonging: Singing together creates a sense of community and shared purpose
  • Cultural Expression: The song reflects our Islamic heritage and Sierra Leonean identity
  • Pride and Spirit: Students develop pride in their school when they sing our anthem
  • Memorable Moments: The song is woven into our most important school memories and traditions

When We Sing Our School Song

Students encounter the school song in many important contexts throughout their BISS journey:

  • At morning assemblies on special occasions
  • During opening and closing ceremonies
  • At prizegiving and graduation ceremonies
  • At school events and celebrations
  • During inter-house competitions
  • At sports days and athletic competitions
  • When BISS teams or delegations represent the school publicly
  • At reunion events bringing together alumni and the school community

Building School Spirit Through Music

Music plays a vital role in school culture at BISS. Beyond the school song, we maintain a strong musical tradition through our school band and various musical programmes. These musical activities help develop students' creativity, discipline, and appreciation for the arts while strengthening the bonds within our school community.
