Examinations & Assessments

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School uses comprehensive examination and assessment procedures to evaluate student learning, progress, and achievement. Our assessment systems are designed to support student growth and identify areas for improvement.

Types of Assessment

Continuous Assessment

  • Class tests and quizzes
  • Class participation and engagement
  • Homework and assignments
  • Projects and practical work
  • Contributes to overall grade calculation

Terminal Examinations

  • Mid-term examinations (held during term)
  • End-of-term final examinations
  • End-of-year comprehensive exams
  • Formal written and practical assessments

Public Examinations

  • Form 3 assessments
  • WASSCE (West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination)
  • International examinations (where applicable)

Assessment Policies

  • All assessments are designed to measure learning outcomes
  • Assessments are fair, valid, and reliable
  • Students are informed of assessment criteria in advance
  • Regular feedback is provided to support improvement
  • Academic integrity and honesty are strictly enforced

Grading System

Student performance is reported using a standardized grading system that reflects achievement levels and provides clear feedback to parents and students.

Examination Conduct

Students are expected to:

  • Arrive on time with proper identification
  • Follow all examination rules and regulations
  • Complete assessments independently
  • Maintain academic honesty
  • Maintain silence and respect other candidates

Support & Accommodations

Students with special needs or learning differences may be eligible for assessment accommodations. Please contact the school for information about available support.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
