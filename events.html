School Events & Celebrations

Throughout the academic year, Benevolent Islamic Secondary School organizes various events and celebrations that bring the school community together. These events foster unity, celebrate achievements, and create lasting memories.

Annual School Events

  • Opening Ceremony: Marks the beginning of the academic year with speeches and community-building activities
  • Speech Day/Awards Ceremony: Celebrates student achievements across academics, sports, and character
  • Sports Day: Highlights athletic competitions and house rivalries
  • Cultural Day: Celebrates diverse cultural traditions and talents
  • Graduation Ceremony: Celebrates students completing their studies with us
  • Inter-house Competitions: Foster friendly competition across various activities

Islamic Celebrations

The school celebrates important Islamic occasions including:

  • Mawlid (Prophet's Birthday)
  • Eid-ul-Fitr
  • Eid-ul-Adha
  • Islamic Awareness Week

Academic Events

  • Science and Mathematics Fairs
  • Debate Competitions
  • Inter-school Academic Competitions
  • Book Fair

Social & Community Events

  • End-of-Term Celebrations
  • Community Service Days
  • Parent-School Social Events
  • Alumni Reunion

Event Calendar

A comprehensive school calendar listing all major events is published at the beginning of each academic year. Updates are communicated through school announcements and the school website.

Community Participation

Parents, guardians, and community members are welcome to participate in school events. These occasions provide an excellent opportunity to celebrate our school community and support our students.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
