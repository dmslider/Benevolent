Life at School

Life at Benevolent Islamic Secondary School extends far beyond the classroom. We believe in developing the whole studentâ€”intellectually, socially, spiritually, and physically. Our vibrant school community offers numerous opportunities for students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and form lasting friendships.

Daily School Activities

Morning Assembly

Every school day begins with a morning assembly where the school community gathers to start the day with inspirational messages, important announcements, and collective prayer. Assembly fosters a sense of unity and shared values.

Sports & Physical Education

We offer a comprehensive sports program that promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition. Students participate in various sporting activities including football, athletics, basketball, and other sports.

House System

Students are organized into houses that foster healthy competition and community spirit. Each house organizes activities and competes in sports, academics, and cultural events throughout the year.

Extracurricular Programs

  • Afterschool Clubs - Various clubs including debate, science, arts, and technology
  • School Band - Music and performance opportunities
  • School Song - Cultural traditions and musical heritage
  • Academic competitions and quizzes
  • Community service and outreach programs
  • Islamic studies and religious education

Library & Learning Resources

Our comprehensive School Library provides students with extensive resources for independent learning, research projects, and recreational reading. The library is a quiet, supportive environment for academic work.

Technology & Computing

The Computer Laboratory is equipped with modern technology enabling students to develop digital literacy skills, conduct research, and engage with contemporary learning tools.

Student Support Services

We provide comprehensive support services including academic counseling, pastoral care, and guidance to ensure every student has the support they need to thrive.

School Events & Celebrations

Throughout the year, we organize various Events and celebrations that bring the school community together. These include cultural days, award ceremonies, sports days, and end-of-term celebrations.

About BISS

Benevolent Islamic Secondary School is committed to providing quality education rooted in Islamic values, preparing students for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.
